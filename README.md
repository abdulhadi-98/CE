# Corporate Entrepreneurship (CE)

This repository contains educational materials for a Corporate Entrepreneurship course based on the book "Burns 2020 Corporate Entrepreneurship and Innovation.pdf".

## Contents

- **Burns 2020 Corporate Entrepreneurship and Innovation.pdf** - Main textbook
- **Chapter_3_Innovation_Lecture_Notes.md** - Comprehensive lecture notes for Chapter 3: Innovation

## Chapter 3: Innovation - Lecture Notes

The lecture notes for Chapter 3 provide a comprehensive, PhD-level academic resource for teaching innovation in corporate entrepreneurship. The notes include:

### Key Topics Covered:
1. Purpose and importance of innovation
2. Defining and measuring innovation (product, process, marketing, business model)
3. Radical vs. incremental innovation
4. Business model innovation and market paradigm shifts
5. Creativity and innovation management
6. Innovation intensity and its measurement
7. Innovation risk management
8. Relationship between innovation, profitability, and growth
9. Innovation across company sizes and industry structures

### Features:
- **Learning Outcomes**: Clear objectives for student comprehension
- **Theoretical Frameworks**: Including Schumpeter's types, Blue Ocean Strategy, Service-Dominant Logic
- **Real-World Examples**: Apple, Tesla, Swatch, low-cost airlines, Caterpillar, and more
- **Case Studies**: 5 detailed case studies for in-depth analysis
- **Discussion Questions**: 8 categories of thought-provoking questions
- **Class Activities**: 6 interactive exercises with detailed instructions
- **Recommended Reading**: 11 key academic and contemporary sources
- **Teaching Notes**: Lecture delivery recommendations and assessment suggestions

### Format:
The lecture notes are structured in a professional academic format suitable for:
- PhD-level instruction
- MBA courses
- Executive education
- Corporate training programs

### Usage:
These notes are designed to be used alongside the main textbook to deliver a comprehensive Chapter 3 lecture series (recommended: 3 x 90-minute sessions).
